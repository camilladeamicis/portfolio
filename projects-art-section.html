"NOCE"
The painted walnut is 10 times bigger than the real (30cm - 3 cm) one and I’ve decided to paint it without the aid of any instrument (no photo, no digital zoom, no magnifying glass), apart from my eyes. Enlargement was therefore invented.
OIL COLOR ON CANVAS
50 X 50 CM. 2021
"CINQUINA"
This car was parked in Melfi, Italy. I decided to paint it with a focus on chrome. In fact, if you look closer, on the headlight you will find the church of Melfi, on the bumber the houses of the city and on the rim, the rust.

"SALUTI, LUCA"
An inordinate interest in chrome materials, mirrors, and curved surfaces led me straight here: to a one meter x half a meter painting of this bike I hold in my heart.
If you look closer, this is the painting of the garden around the motorcycle, of the person taking the photo and the sky, all reflected.
OIL COLOR ON CANVAS
50 X 100 CM. 2019
"BOE"
This painting resembles the reflection of a boat in the sea. If you are born at the sea, the sea is. It is a part, a whole, it is space, freedom and belonging. In this case a sea that speaks of home, of port and slow bike rides while whistling, of Romagna dialect and smell of salt.
OIL COLOR ON CANVAS
60 X 60 CM. 2019
"CALPESTARE"
The shoes here depicted have been around with me for years, until they were destroyed. I started to paint as a way to fix the amount of life they have been through, shown in their old leather.
